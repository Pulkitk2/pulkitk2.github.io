About Me

Based in New Delhi, I am an IIT Kharagpur post-graduate (Batch of 2018), actively seeking job opportunities as a patent analyst. My graduation and post-graduation were in biotechnology and agricultural biotechnology, respectively. I keenly follow new developments in the field of science, especially biotechnology. It is this passion for reading scientific literature that drew me towards the world of patents. So, I followed up my post-graduation with a certification from Indian Law Institute in Intellectual Property Rights (IPR). I am currently actively seeking for opportunities in the role of patent search and analysis.

Apart from reading science news and research articles, I like to read about environmental issues and Indian history as well. Being well-informed about the scientific aspects of pollution and climate change, I am deeply concerned about the future of this planet. I am a strong advocate of sustainable development and inclusive growth.

Another hobby of mine is writing and producing music. I make complete rap songs (both lyrics and music) all by myself. I have a YouTube channel under the stage name of Mir Atish. I mostly write about social issues which I like to think are important and relatable to my audience. I also try to share my knowledge through my songs so that it becomes accessible to more people.
